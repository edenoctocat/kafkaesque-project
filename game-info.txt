the "object" of the game is for you, '@', to travel through the rooms, 
collecting coins, '$'. the coins can be used for the tolls which you must pay 
when moving into another room, and to buy food, which replenishes your energy. 

energy: 
your energy starts at 10, and when it reaches 0, the game ends. each food item
costs 2 coins and gives you 2 energy levels.  

   press b to buy food.

movement:

   press w to move up. 
   press s to move down. 
   press a to move left. 
   press d to move right. 

other keys:

   press h for help. 
   press q to quit.

press enter to continue...
